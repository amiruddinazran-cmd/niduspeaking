About the Programme

This 3 months programme is designed to help school students build confidence in speaking, develop logical thinking, and express ideas clearly in front of others.

The course combines engaging hands-on practice, guided feedback, and age-appropriate activities to ensure measurable progress.

What Students Will Learn

Confident Speaking

Overcome stage fright and speak clearly in front of an audience.

Structured Thinking

Organise ideas logically and present arguments with clarity.

Persuasive Communication

Learn how to influence, explain, and engage listeners effectively.

Real-World Skills

Useful for class presentations, debates, interviews, and leadership roles.
